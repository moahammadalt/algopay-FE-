Grow your business with AlgoPay. Become a vendor.

AlgoPay partners with local businesses to drive foot traffic to their
stores while helping to build the Algo community.

Get paid to bring in new customers with AlgoPay. Here’s how.



Algo Vendors serve as our human ATMs. We pay you monthly to let our users deposit and withdraw
cash into your register. Over half of AlgoPay users shop where they withdraw.

Apply to become an Algo Vendor location. If you’re selected, we pay you.

Your register becomes an Algo ATM. We let our users know that they can deposit & withdraw cash at your store.

Your store brings in new customers as AlgoPay users come in to deposit or withdraw funds.

Follow the steps on your AlgoPay app to confirm each transaction from your phone.
