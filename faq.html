Got questions? We’ve got answers.

AlgoPay is your digital wallet, designed so that you could access it easily and from anywhere. We strive to make financial storage a tool for everyone.



Using your account.

Do I need a bank accout to use AlgoPay?


No, you do not need a bank account to use AlgoPay. In fact, AlgoPay was created to easily take the place of any long-term banking solution. We let users deposit and withdraw money into their account and access it from any Algo Vendor, so no banking access required.



How do I deposit money into my account?


You can deposit money into your account at any Algo Vendor location. Seclect on your app how much you would like to deposit, and follow the steps. Bring your app to any cashier at the nearest Algo Vendor location, and deposit your cash into the register. Your new balance will show up within one hour, and you can access those funds from any location we operate in.



How do I withdraw money from my account?


You can withdraw your funds in cash at any Algo Vendor location. Select on your app how much you would like to withdraw, and follow the steps. Bring your app to any cashier at the nearest Algo Vendor location, and withdraw straight from the register. Your new balance will show up within one hour. Note that you are only able to withdraw funds that currently show up on your balance.



Where can I withdraw my cash?


AlgoPay currently has vendor locations in Istanbul, Beirut and Amman. We select vendors that are in convenient locations; at grocery stores, pharmacies, etc. If you don’t have an Algo Vendor near you, let us know here! We’d love to bring our service to you. Search for Algo Vendors in your city here.

Algo Vendors.

What is an Algo Vendor?


You can think of Algo Vendors as human ATMs. We partner with small businesses in your city to make it easier for you to deposit and withdraw cash from anywhere. You can bring your app to any Algo Vendor to deposit or withdraw cash, on the spot. Note: every Algo Vendor location is listed on your app. If a business is not listed, they are not a certified Algo Vendor and should not be used to deposit and withdraw funds.

You are encouraged to report fraudulent Algo Vendors here.



What do Algo Vendors do?


Algo Vendors are the front-line of AlgoPay. Our Vendors are small businesses that let AlgoPay users withdraw and deposit funds into their registers. If you are selected to work as an Algo Vendor, your business will experience a significant increase in local foot traffic, driven by our team. All you have to do is provide top-notch customer service and provide our users with a safe and clean environment to deposit and withdraw funds from your register. Top Vendors receive bonus perks to help you grow your business.



Am I qualified to be an Algo Vendor?


We welcome any small business to apply to partner with us as an Algo Vendor. We will evaluate applications based on location, business reviews, and proximity to our users. Apply to become an Algo Vendor here.

Where we work.

What countries does AlgoPay operate in?


AlgoPay has Vendor locations in Beirut, Istanbul and Amman. We’re working to expand to where our users are. If you’d like to use AlgoPay and don’t see a Vendor near you, let us know here.



I just moved to a new country. Can I still access my AlgoPay account?


Yes! We’ve designed AlgoPay to be a totally borderless money sotrage solution. Moving to a new city or country will never freeze your account, and you can always access your funds by going to your local Algo Vendor location. If AlgoPay doesn’t operate in the country you’re moving to our visiting, let us know here.
